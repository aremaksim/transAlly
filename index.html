About This Website

Find your trans health care informations here!

This is how this page works:

People can make suggestions for doctors that were transfriendly and experienced in working with trans people and knowing and understanding the needs of trans people. Once a suggestion for a source has been voted for a minimum of 3 times it will appear on the listings. Everyone can vote up or down for a source someone has shared. This way I want to make sure that no source can be posted without being double checked and verified by at least 2 other queers. The goal is to provide information on where to go as a trans person in need and long term to create a database in the from of an API so other health care organisations can make use of our carefully curated database and spread this essential information to other people in need.

There is also a section for recommended books by our community. Go and have a look and if you like also vote if you like or dislike a suggestion. These entries won't be deleted.
